About Us

Hello, we are Adopt-Me. Adopt-Me is a organization for cat adoption and cat rescue located in Jakarta. We help these cats get a home. We use Internet to help these cats get adopters. So, we connect adopters with shelter and help cats go from alone to adopted.

Why Adoption?

Millions of cat that adoptable are killed because overpopulation. This thing happen because the number of cat adoptions in need are compounded by a surplus of cats bred just for profit. If you taking home a cat from a shelter even a rescue, not only you are saving that pet, you're either making room in the rescue so they can save another pet from a shelter, or making room at the shelter itself.
